The Four Key Stages of Food Processing

1. Raw Material Handling

The first step in food processing is the careful selection and handling of raw materials, ensuring they are fresh and free from contaminants.

2. Cleaning and Sorting

After handling, materials are cleaned and sorted to remove any impurities, making them safe and ready for the next stage of processing.

3. Cooking and Preservation

Ingredients undergo cooking, boiling, or other preservation methods to enhance flavor, safety, and shelf life of the products.

4. Packaging

Once processed, food items are packaged in eco-friendly materials, ready for distribution and consumption.
